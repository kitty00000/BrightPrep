FAQs

Q1. What should I bring to my exam?
  • Pencils, pens, and erasers
  • A calculator (if required)
  • Your textbook if it hasn't been handed in yet
  • A ruler, protractor, or any other specific tools your teacher mentioned
  • Remember, no cell phones or smartwatches are allowed in the examination room.
Q2. What if I don't understand a question on the exam?
Unfortunately, teachers cannot explain questions during the exam. If you're unsure, make your best attempt and move on, as you can always come back to it later.
Q3. What should I do if I'm severely sick on exam day?
If you're severely sick on exam day, contact your instructor as soon as possible to discuss your options. You may need to provide a medical certificate.
