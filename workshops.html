Workshops

Time Management

Helps students optimize their study time by teaching them to create effective schedules, prioritize tasks, and avoid procrastination. It introduces tools like planners and prioritization techniques, enabling students to balance study and breaks, reduce stress, and boost productivity for exam success.

Stress-Management

A stress-management workshop teaches students relaxation techniques, mindfulness, and healthy habits to handle exam pressure. It provides tools to manage time, reframe negative thoughts, and build resilience, helping them stay calm and focused during exams.

Organize Study Routine

An "Organize Your Study Routine" workshop teaches students to create structured study plans, prioritize tasks, and use tools like planners effectively. It helps them eliminate distractions, balance study with breaks, and stay consistent for better exam preparation.
